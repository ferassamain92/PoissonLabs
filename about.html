About Us

Founded in 2023, we are an early start up Actuarial P&C pricing consultancy based out of Toronto, Canada.

We support small Property & Casualty insurers in small and underserved markets globally.

Our focus is exclusively on Actuarial P&C pricing, centered around three core pillars:

Actuarial Logic for Rating Engines

Build end-to-end rating framework and algorithms to power your premium volume

Predictive Modelling to refine risk pricing

Customized state-of-the art models to predict enhance competitveness and limit mis-pricing risk.

Analytics & Decision Systems

Turn pricing outputs into actionable underwriting and portfolio decision support tools.

We also offer advanced pricing tools. Inquire for more info.

What we are not:We do not build policy systems, databases, or cloud infrastructure. We build the actuarial intelligence that anchors your underwriting profitability. We help you take control of and own your pricing and profitability journey.

Our Mission is to bring sophisticated actuarial pricing techniques to your fingertips, and help you establish a modern & competitive rating framework to support the growth of your business.

Staff: Mix of qualified actuaries with deep expertise, quants, and students
