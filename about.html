About Us

Founded in 2023, we are an early start up consultancy based out of Toronto, Canada.

Exclusively focused on Actuarial P&C pricing.

We support insurers in smaller and underserved markets around the world.

Our practice is based on three core services:

Rating Engines

Create rating frameworks and build Actuarial logic for pricing systems of any peril or line of business.

Modelling

Build sophisticated predictive models to optimize rates, and integrate into your rating engine.

Special Projects

Build advanced pricing tools, conduct a specialized research project, or develop analytics tools to support analysis and decision making.

Our Mission

Bring sophisticated actuarial pricing techniques to your fingertips, and help you establish a modern & competitive rating framework to take control over the growth of your business.
